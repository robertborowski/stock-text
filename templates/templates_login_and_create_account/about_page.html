SymbolNews

About

Purpose: SymbolNews saves you look up time and keeps you up to date on the latest news for the companies you care about.

Problem: My day job keeps me busy and I rarely have time to look at the latest news for the companies I follow. I often find out major company news early the following day, days after the event occurs, or never at all.

Examples of news I have missed in the past:

  • A new person was added to the company board and the stock price went up +5%.

  • The company had a major recall and stock went down -7%.

  • The CEO was involved in a scandal, stock down -8%.

SymbolNews solves this problem.
